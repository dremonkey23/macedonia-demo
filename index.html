Our Story
Rooted in Faith Since 1832
On April 9, 1832, Wade and Marie Mills donated land specifically for the building of a church for worship. Since that day, Macedonia has been a beacon of faith, love, and community in Louisa County.

Now a Global Methodist Church, we remain committed to the Lordship of Jesus Christ, the inspiration and authority of the Scriptures, and the work of the Holy Spirit in conveying God's truth and grace to all people.
